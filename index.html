Just type in the big rectangle.

Just type in the big rectangle. Simple, easy, and fun, BigRectangle is a bunch of free, useful, open-source tools that can be controlled by - quite simply - just typing in the big rectangle.

Plus, over one-third of profits to go a charity that BigRectangle users choose!

Example: You type "When is next week?" BigRectangle then uses ColorCalendar - one of the subtools in BigRectangle - to tell you "Saturday, August 18, 2012 12:00:00 AM"
