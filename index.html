Find tips that work for you.

Predictor aims to help make people better at what they do* by predicting tips that'll work for you. Simply tell Predictor what you're doing and Predictor will automagically show you short tips.

Example: You tell Predictor you're gardening. Predictor shows you gardening tips, like "You can cut open a bag of potting soil and plant almost anything in the bag!" You can click on a tip to learn more.

Aren't there alternatives?

Sure, there are other ways to get better at something. Maybe you'd go to a group of people like you. But those groups can be just as inexperienced as you are, and small too. So maybe you'd read a book about the topic. But books like these can be hundreds of pages long, cost mucho dinero and turn out to be useless. So maybe you'd call an expert. But the chances of them picking up the phone? Not likely.

How does it work?

After you make an account, you go to the tip-entering page, where you enter tips. You assign each tip a topic. People vote on their favorite tips. Once you vote on enough tips, Predictor uses its' special algorithm to find tips that work for you.
